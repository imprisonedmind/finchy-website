Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-19

Finchy is a local‑only personal finance app. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter into the app is stored solely on your device.

  • No external APIs or third‑party SDKs are used.
  • No account creation, logins, or cloud sync.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers.
  • Data access is limited to on‑device storage necessary for core functionality.

If you choose to uninstall the app, all locally stored data is removed by your device as part of the uninstall process. We cannot recover or access your data at any point.
